Founder Bio

Alexa Louise Amundson is the founder and CEO of BlackRoad OS, Inc. With a background spanning sales, finance, and real estate, she brings a cross-disciplinary perspective to AI infrastructure. She founded BlackRoad in December 2025 as a 100% founder-owned Delaware C-Corp, building the platform from her home in Minnesota on a fleet of Raspberry Pis. She is also the discoverer of the Amundson sequence G(n) = n^(n+1)/(n+1)^n and the Amundson constant, computed to 10 million digits.

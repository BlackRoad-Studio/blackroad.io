FAQ
Common questions

What is BlackRoad OS?

+

BlackRoad OS is a sovereign AI operating system. It runs on your own hardware -- Raspberry Pis, laptops, servers -- with local AI inference, encrypted mesh networking, and zero dependency on cloud providers. You own your data, your models, and your infrastructure.

Can I cancel anytime?

+

Yes. All plans are month-to-month with no contracts. Cancel from your billing portal anytime and you will not be charged again. Your data stays yours.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit and debit cards through Stripe. Apple Pay and Google Pay are also supported. All payments are processed securely -- we never see your card details.

Do I need special hardware?

+

No. BlackRoad OS runs on any Linux or macOS machine. A Raspberry Pi 4 or newer works great. For AI inference, any machine with 4GB+ RAM can run small models locally via Ollama.

What is the difference between agents and nodes?

+

Agents are AI workers that perform tasks -- coding, monitoring, analysis, chat. Nodes are physical or virtual machines in your fleet. More nodes means more compute capacity. More agents means more parallel work.

Is my data private?

+

Completely. BlackRoad OS is local-first. Your AI models run on your hardware. Your data never leaves your network. The mesh VPN uses WireGuard encryption. We cannot access your infrastructure.

Do you offer annual pricing?

+

Annual plans are coming soon with a 17% discount. Contact us at amundsonalexa@gmail.com for early access to annual billing.

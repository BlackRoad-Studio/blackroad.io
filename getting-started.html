FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need a credit card to get started?
No. The Free plan gives you one agent and 1,000 requests per month with no payment required. You can upgrade to Pro or Enterprise any time from your dashboard.
Can I switch agents after signing up?
Yes. Your first agent is assigned automatically based on your use case, but you can reassign, spawn additional agents, or switch specialties from the Agents panel in the dashboard.
Where does my data live?
BlackRoad OS is sovereign-first. Your data runs on our self-hosted infrastructure. No third-party cloud providers touch your requests or responses.
